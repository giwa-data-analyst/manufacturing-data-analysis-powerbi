# 📊Manufacturing Data Analysis (Power BI Project)

## 🔍Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end data analysis solution for a manufacturing company using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into production performance, downtime trends, operational efficiency, and product quality.

The goal is to transform raw manufacturing data into actionable insights that support decision-making and improve operational performance.

## 🧠Business Problem

Manufacturing companies often struggle with:

- High downtime affecting productivity
- Inefficient production processes
- Quality defects impacting customer satisfaction
- Lack of clear performance visibility across plants

This project addresses these challenges by providing a centralized dashboard for monitoring and analysis.

## 📊Dashboard Features

#### 1. Executive Overview

- Total Production & Revenue tracking
- Efficiency and Defect KPIs
- Monthly revenue trend
- Production distribution by plant

#### 2. Quality & Defect Analysis

- Defect rate trend over time
- Total defects by product
- Quality performance insights
- Identification of high-defect products

#### 3. Downtime & Operational Risk

- Monthly downtime trend
- Downtime comparison (MoM)
- Downtime contribution by plant
- Operational risk insights

#### 4. Efficiency Performance

- Units per downtime hour
- Efficiency trend analysis
- Performance comparison across plants

## 📈Key Insights

- 📉 Downtime increased from January to March before improving in April
- ⚠️ Higher downtime is linked to reduced operational efficiency
- 🏭 Plant A contributes more to total downtime, indicating potential inefficiencies
- 📊 Defect rate remains around the threshold, requiring continuous monitoring
- 🚀 Efficiency performance shows strong potential when downtime is controlled

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Excel / CSV (Data Source)
